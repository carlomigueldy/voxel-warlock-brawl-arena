TUTORIAL

Learn the rules, master your spells, last warlock wins.

No health bars
Each hit adds charge to the target. The higher the charge, the farther they fly on the next hit — knock them off the platform to eliminate them.
Charge mechanics
Base knockback 8. Every hit adds +0.14 charge (cap 4.0). Extra impulse = charge × 11. Charge decays at 0.05/s while you're alive.
Hazard timer
Fall into the hazard below and you have 3.5 s to return. Stay too long and you're eliminated for that round.
Fall-stun
Drop off a ledge ≥ 1.5 u tall and you're stunned 2 s on landing — no movement, firing, or casting. Ramps never trigger it.
High ground matters
Shots fired downhill always connect. Shots fired uphill often skim under targets. Tall obstacles also block projectiles — use cover.
Win condition
Last warlock standing wins the round. First to 5 round wins wins the match. Supports up to 6 warlocks.
Arena shrink
Platform starts at radius 18 (medium). After 8 s it shrinks at 0.30 u/s until it reaches minimum radius 6. Don't back into a shrinking edge.
Round start
A 3 s countdown precedes every round. Use it to orient yourself and plan your opening.

  • Build charge first. Let Fireball stack charge on an enemy before throwing a high-knockback spell — a fully charged target can be launched off the platform in one hit.
  • Hold the high ground. Plateaus let your shots connect downhill while incoming fire skims beneath you. Reach them via ramps — not ledges (fall-stun).
  • Watch the shrink. The arena starts shrinking after 8 s. Back-pedaling into a shrinking edge is a self-elimination.
  • Counter with Shield. One Shield absorbs the next hit entirely — save it to eat a Meteor or Homing bolt at full charge and keep your position.
  • Teleport off hazards. If you land in lava or ocean, Teleport back to the platform before the 3.5 s death timer runs out.
  • Drain + Gravity combo. Pull an enemy close with Drain, then drop a Gravity well — they can't escape and will sail off the edge.
  • Time Shift safety valve. Activate before a risky dive; if things go wrong it rewinds your position 3 s back to safety.
  • Pocket Watch on match point. Save this rare CD reset for round 5 — instantly clearing all cooldowns can flip the entire brawl.
  • Stay mobile. Move speed is 9 u/s. A stationary warlock is easy prey for Homing bolts and Meteor strikes.
  • Rush through knockback. Activate Rush before a fight to shrug off knockback by 45 % and sprint ahead — great for holding the centre when the arena shrinks.

    The Warlock's Code

    Every warlock stepped into this arena to play. Keep it that way.

    • Respect everyone. No racism, hate speech, slurs, or harassment — ever. There's no version of this that's a joke.
    • Taunt, don't trash-talk. Playful jabs, cocky banter, and a little smack talk are part of the fun. Cruelty, personal attacks, and toxic pile-ons are not.
    • Put the aggression in your spells, not your words. Play hard, aim to win, keep it clean.
    • Win with grace, lose with grit. Be the opponent you'd actually want to queue into — both directions.

    Voice and chat are a shared arena. You can mute anyone, anytime — it's instant, private, and they're never told. Break the Code and the arena isn't for you.
